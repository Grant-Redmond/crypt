A Home For Your Digital Currency(ies)

CRYPT is a simple, clean and user friendly Bitcoin wallet. We aren't a centralized service like a lot of wallets and exchanges out there. We NEVER touch or see yoru funds. We simply connect you to the Bitcoin network. Just download and open up your new CRYPT wallet and it generates your unique Bitcoin address & recovery phrase. Your address is allows you to receive payments and your phrase (write down and keep this secret!) allows you to restore or transfer your wallet if you were to accidentally delete it.

Protect your digital assest. BTC, ETH, USDT, DAO, DASH, LTC, DOGE; Everything At The Tip of Your Fingers. That's our goal! We want to be your Cryptocurrency/Blockchain hub. From Bitcoin to Ethereum, we are aiming to integrate seamless and frictionless access to all your digital assets. CRYPT is currently in development with 1.0 coming very soon! We are always looking for talented cryptocurrency/blockchain developers. If you've got experience in the space, we'd love to hear from you.
