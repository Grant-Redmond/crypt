A Home For Your Digital Currency(ies)

CRYPT is a simple, clean and user friendly Bitcoin wallet. We aren't a centralized service like a lot of wallets and exchanges out there. We NEVER touch or see your funds (feel free to dig through our source code). We simply connect you to the Bitcoin network. Just download and install the CRYPT Wallet Beta and it generates your unique Bitcoin address & recovery phrase. Your address/QR code allows you to receive payments and your phrase (write down and keep this secret!) allows you to restore or transfer your wallet. CRYPT is currently in Beta for Windows & Mac (Linux Soon), so please only store/move small amounts until full release

Protect your digital assest. BTC, ETH, USDT, DAO, DASH, LTC, DOGE; Everything At The Tip of Your Fingers. That's our goal! We want to be your Cryptocurrency/Blockchain hub.
